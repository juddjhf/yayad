Class 12 Accountancy Overview

üßæ What is Accountancy?

Accountancy (or accounting) is the systematic process of recording, measuring, processing, and communicating financial transactions of a business or organization.

It serves to:

  • Track business performance (profit & loss)
  • Present financial position (assets, liabilities, equity)
  • Inform stakeholders for decision-making

üìö Why is Accountancy Used?

  • Decision Support: Helps management and investors make informed financial decisions.
  • Compliance: Statements follow legal standards like Schedule III or GAAP.
  • Transparency: Provides credible info to regulators, banks, shareholders.
  • Performance Analysis: Ratios and cash flow show profitability & liquidity.
